About

The dashboard is presented with a login page where the electricity incharges could register themselves. After registration, depending upon the chosen wind speed and direction, the energy to be generated from the wind turbine is displayed as a prediction result.

Feature of Our model

Business / Social impact

    The proposed model is useful for correctly predicting the power generation. If the power generation is predicted accurately, then the power supply to the public is planned in advance. The proposed system helps to analyze the supply chain relationship. The cost of unnecessary operation of the turbine in poor weather conditions is reduced. This system is useful to the public electricity management board in analyzing wind power generation. It helps in reducing the power generation cost at an utmost level
